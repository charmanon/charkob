What's happening?

A long time ago, in a city college far far away in Pasadena, two nerds met in an engineering club and eventually became friends. Some time later, after one shared class, innumerable CAPS-LOCKED texts, a garage metal concert in a stranger's house, tearful heart-to-hearts, and a generous amount of watermelon vodka, those two nerds eventually figured things out and leveled up their friendship.

Fast forward five years to the present, they still like each other a lot and figure they could share four score years or more together in their shared weirdness.

Those two people are actually us (Charlotte and Jakob), if you haven't figured that out yet. What we're trying to say is that we're gonna have a marriage ceremony.

When and where will the party get started?

On Saturday March 31st, 2018 our honored guests are cordially invited to a ceremony that will bind our two families together by the will of a higher power: the US government.

The ceremony will be held in the beautifully maintained Club at Five Knolls ™ (see map below) and it will start at 4 pm in the afternoon, barring any unforseen inclement weather such as car-sized hail, shark tornadoes, and light sprinkles.

If the spirit is willing, the party can last all night, but since we have to go to bed on time to watch Jeopardy re-runs, it'll most likely end by 10pm.

Parking is available at the lot adjacent to the Club and on the streets marked red. Don't worry about the No Stopping signs posted, Five Knolls is used for overflow parking all the time.
