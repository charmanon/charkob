What's happening?

A long time ago, in a city college far far away in Pasadena, two nerds met in an engineering club and eventually became friends. Some time later, after one shared class, innumerable CAPS-LOCKED texts, a garage metal concert in a stranger's house, tearful heart-to-hearts, and a generous amount of watermelon vodka, those two nerds eventually figured things out and leveled up their friendship.

Fast forward five years to the present, they still like each other a lot and figure they could share four score years or more together in their shared weirdness.

Those two people are actually us (Charlotte and Jakob), if you haven't figured that out yet. What we're trying to say is that we're gonna have a marriage ceremony.

When and where will the party get started?

On Saturday March 31st, 2018 our honored guests are cordially invited to a ceremony that will bind our two families together by the will of a higher power: the US government.

The ceremony will be held in the beautifully maintained Club at Five Knolls ™ at 20551 Bentwood Ln, Santa Clarita, CA. The ceremony will start at 4 pm in the afternoon, barring any unforseen inclement weather such as car-sized hail, shark tornadoes, and light sprinkles.

If the spirit is willing, the party can last all night, but since we have to go to bed on time to watch Jeopardy re-runs, it'll most likely end by 10pm.

Parking is available at the lot adjacent to the Club and on the streets marked red. Don't worry about the No Stopping signs posted, Five Knolls is used for overflow parking all the time.

FAQ

Can I wear a pokemon onesie/Renaissance costume/vaguely videogame inspired formal wear?

The theme of the wedding is FUN! There will be lots of activities for everyone and it'll be better if you can move around freely. We encourage bright bold colors and any videogame/cartoon inspired clothing. Or even a costume if you'd like. If you insist on wearing formal clothing, you'll be given something in keeping with the theme. If you'd like a visual cue for what to wear, check out this pinterest board!

 Can I bring my dog/cat/iguana?

While we would love to have all creatures big and small attend our wedding, the venue is sadly not equipped to handle Princess Poochie. Plus why would you traumatize them with loud and drunk humans?

 Can I bring a plus one?

We wanted to invite everyone we knew, but the venue can only hold so many bodies (75 to be exact). We'd like our closest family and friends there so we've invited people we know. We can make judgement calls of your plus one though, so call or text us at (818)281-0834 (Charlotte) or (818)425-1924 (Jakob).
